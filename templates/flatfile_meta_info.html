Ground motion data are commonly stored in a tabular data file called Flatfile. In eGSIM, flatfiles are used to compare observed or synthetic ground motions with the model predictions implemented in OpenQuake. To do so, each flatfile record must include the ground motions (intensity measures such as PGA) alongside the input parameters (e.g., magnitude) required by the models.

As such, the data to supply in an eGSIM flatfile depend on the models to compare and the intensity measures to use. In order to help the nontrivial compilation of a flatfile, often from heterogeneous external sources, you can select here the models and intensity measures of interest and dynamically generate a comprehensive documentation of the required flatfile fields. Once compiled, flatfiles can be uploaded in HDF (recommended) or CSV format and used throughout the application (Data visual inspection and Model-to-data comparison)
